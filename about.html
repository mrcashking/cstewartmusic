BIOGRAPHY:

Cordaro Uveil Stewart (born Sept 18, 1990), better known by his stage name C. Stewart is an American rap artist and producer.

Cordaro was born in Detroit, MI. He grew up listening to hip-hop, and R&B music at home.

Cordaro was also apart of a Hip-hop and R&B duo group called Famous Freysh with his younger brother Freysh Prince. 

Through out his career he's carried many different stage names such as Famous UveE,

Cash King UveE and Mr. Cash King, later on he decided to settle on just using his birth name. 

He was influenced by musicians such as Nas, Eminem, Royce Da 5'9', Twista, Ludacris and Busta Rhymes.
